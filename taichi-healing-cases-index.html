Cases of Tai Chi Healing

The articles given in at this section are written by people who had various health problems. They write to express their happiness and gratitude for what they have gained and the unexpected health benefits after they learnt from Master Xue the Qi Circulation Techniques of Taichi from the system of Traditional Li branch Taichi (the ancient Li's Taichi). Please note that these articles are translated from their original Chinese versions.

My Taichi master Xue Wenyu, is an inheritor of multiple branches of Taichi and Taoism. He is the 5th generation inheritor of the traditional Li branch Taichi, which is officially recognized as one of the National Intangible Cultural Heritage Assets. He is the 36th generation inheritor of the Southern Branch of Taoist (Qingcheng Dan Dao), the 5th generation inheritor of Youlong Tai Chi, a secret branch of Wudang.

Master Xue established Wenyu Culture Media (Beijing) Co., Ltd. in 2018, which is registered in Chaoyang District, Beijing. He seeks to spread Traditional Li Branch Tai Chi and its culture for the benefit of as many people as possible. With his enterprise-oriented developmental approach and management model, together with self-media platforms and modern teaching concepts, what was a previously hidden treasure, the original Li branch Taichi, has been disseminated to many thousands of people across 28 provinces of the country.
