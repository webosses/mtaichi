Case 3 Diabetes, Heart disease, High blood pressure, Tinnitus, Insomnia

  • Name: Li Xiulan
  • Region: Jiuquan, Gansu

My name is Li Xiulan and I was born in April 1946. Before practicing Tai Chi, I weighed 82 kg, had heart disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, lumbar disc stenosis compressing the nerves, causing my left leg to drop and I had difficulty in moving. I had to walk and stop, and I was hospitalized for treatment many times but it didn't heal. My blood sugar was greater than 9 points and I had to be hospitalized to lower my blood sugar. I had severe tinnitus and insomnia.

When I went to hospital the doctors told me it couldn't be cured. Yet, since I started to learn the Qi Circulation Techniques of Taichi, as passed on by Master Xue Wenyu. from Teacher Ge on May 25, 2019, my body has undergone earth-shattering changes.

The first change is that I used to stay in the hospital three times every six months but now I haven't been to the hospital for more than a year. The second change is that my weight has dropped from 82 kg to the current 72 kg. The third change is that my blood pressure has gone back down to normal and I don't need to take heart disease medicine anymore. The fourth change is that the fasting blood sugar measurement has dropped to 5.3~5.8, and the hypoglycemic medicine has also been reduced. The fifth change is that tinnitus and insomnia have been cured without treatment.
