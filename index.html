The goal of Mtaichi Courses is to help people to master the ancient and venerable art of Tai Chi and thereby improve their health and happiness. We hope that they in turn will further spread the knowledge of these practices to those they love and care for.

Traditional Taichi Training

Wuji Standing Meditation

This ancient practice from Taosim helps the body and mind to be fully relaxed and helps restore the body's original healing power. It helps to improve digestion, relieves constipation, improve circulation, sleep quality and has many other physical and mental benefits.

(sessions required: combined with other courses)

Qi Circulating Techniques of Taichi

This exercise is said been passed down from the patriarch Zhang Sanfeng of Wudang Mountain. It focuses on the Qi around the body, the great Zhoutian, and critical energy switches. By practicing will help the body to build up Qi(energy) and circulating Qi(energy) all around the body. It has been scientifically demonstrated to have multiple health benefits including the relieve of chronic back pain, stabilising blood glucose, lowering blood pressure, reversing arthritis, improving sleep quality, etc.

(12 sessions required to finish this course)

Five Steps and Eight Forces

This ancient practice is the most critical foundation of Tai Chi, It helps practitioners to experience the fundamental core of what Tai Chi actually is. It also helps to build up the core energy of the body and to enhance the potentials within human physiology.

(12 sessions required to finish this course)

Tranditional Tai Chi Form

Traditional Taichi Form is rigorous in structure, smooth in movement, peaceful and calm in mind. It consists 37 unique and 48 repeat movements. Practice in this Tai Chi Form can help to achieve harmony between body and mind, prevent chronic diseases, improve immunity, delay aging and promote physical and mental health

(about 30 sessions required to finish this course)

About Me

I am a 12th. generation inheritor of Chen style Taijiquan Small Frame and a 13th. generation inheritor of Chen style Taijiquan Large Frame .

I am currently a sixth level Tai Chi practitioner, as certified by Hunan Province Wushu Association in 2017. I am also a certified Tai Chi teacher.

After learning and practicing Chen style Tai Chi for many years, I've been attracted to Taoist Taichi in recent years and, by sheer luck, I had the chance to examine more deeply the ancient training system which for centuries was kept in the Lee Family and passed down by my Taichi master, Xue Wen Yu. I am fascinated by the efficient way it cultivates Qi(energy), which speeds up the journey of Tai Chi. The health benefits seems almost magical - many people with serious illnesses have significantly improved by practicing Wuji standing meditation, Qi Circulating Techniques of Tai Chi. Following my Taichi Master, Xue Wen Yu, who wishes to spread these ancient arts and benefits to as many people as possible, together with my personal desire to help people, I would like to share what I have learned with any others who may wish to delve deeper into these ancient arts.
